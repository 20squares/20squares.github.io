EthCC < > QPL research roundtable

This year, both ETHcc[6], Europe's biggest crypto conference, and QPL 2023, one of the leading quantum computing academic conferences, are scheduled to happen in Paris on the same dates, with locations only 15 minutes apart on foot.

...So we decided we had to do a thing about it.

  • Why: This incredible stroke of luck gives us a great opportunity to foster inter-disciplinary collaborations between our respective research fields, crypto and quantum.
  • What: A research roundtable. Main topics will include quantum algos for MEV search, one-shot quantum signatures for improving PoS infra, wider adoption of compositional techniques in crypto, etc.
  • How: Format is open. We talk maths and we chill. There will be a projector and whiteboards if you wanna give a presentation. There will be snacks and coffe as well.
  • Who: The roundtable is open to everyone participating to either EthCC or QPL 2023. It will be rather mathsy and technical, so consider yourself advised.
  • When: July 21st, from 10:30AM till 12:30AM, with the possibility of continuing over lunch.
  • Where: Flashbots' Hacker House, Rue Bla N. 666, Paris.
