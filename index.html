About

20[ ] - also typeset and read 20squares - is a collective specializing in economic and financial modelling in the crypto space.

We employ a specific tool called Compositional Game Theory, which we defined and implemented ourselves. We specialize in:

  • Modelling incentive mechanisms for staking protocols and computing their cost of equilibrium - e.g. how much do you have to bribe a validator(s) to deviate from intended behavior?
  • Finding advanced economic exploits - e.g. when does a change in a given contract result in an exploit opportunity opening in some seemingly unrelated other contract?
  • Finding the economic bounds in which a contract operates as intended - e.g. in which market conditions actors lose interest in defending a stablecoin's peg?
  • All stuff concerning MEV.

We may be open to some collaborative/consulting work, provided that the problem you have is interesting enough.
